# MapPi3 V1.2.21 Sunrise/Sunset Leave Planner

## Added

- Approximate route-trailhead sunrise and sunset for selected hike date.
- Sun target selector: sunrise, sunset, or custom target time.
- Arrive-early buffer in minutes.
- Leave-trailhead time calculator: target time minus arrive-early buffer minus route duration.
- Current/home reference to trailhead straight-line distance and rough drive time estimate.
- Hike plan export includes sunrise, sunset, target, leave time, travel distance, and rough drive time.
- Launch checklist includes a sunrise/sunset planning row.
- Navigate can show both turn-around and leave-time badges after starting from Launch.

## Safety

Sunrise/sunset is approximate client-side math for the route coordinate. Travel distance is straight-line, not road routing. Validate road drive time, parking access, local sunrise/sunset, trail conditions, and weather before depending on it.
