# MapPi3 V1.1.4 Hiker Health Notes

## Purpose

V1.1.4 adds the first dedicated **Health** section for hiker profile, intake, vitals, condition-aware effort, and real-time hike estimates.

## Added

- New `Health` tab.
- Hiker profile fields:
  - name
  - birthday via calendar/date input
  - weight in pounds
  - height in inches
- Intake fields:
  - food calories consumed
  - water consumed in ounces
- Vitals fields:
  - heart rate
  - blood pressure systolic/diastolic
  - pulse timer helper: beats counted over seconds, converted to BPM
- Condition inputs:
  - temperature
  - humidity
  - air quality index
  - elevation
- Real-time estimates tied to current simulated hike progress:
  - calories burned
  - normal-walk calorie comparison
  - net calories
  - water target and water balance
  - pace
  - MET estimate
  - condition-adjusted effort score

## Calculation approach

The current model uses a field estimate based on:

- hiker body weight
- elapsed time
- simulated miles progressed
- route elevation gain
- temperature
- humidity
- elevation
- current/entered pulse and heart-rate context

This gives rough comparison and trend feedback, not clinical precision.

## Health safety boundary

MapPi3 should never claim exact muscle gain, diagnose health conditions, or replace a doctor, trainer, nutritionist, or medical device. Muscle growth is not directly measurable from one hike in the app. Instead, MapPi3 tracks workload, calories, hydration, heart-rate response, conditions, and consistency over time.

## Future health work
