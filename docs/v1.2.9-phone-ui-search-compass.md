# MapPi3 V1.2.9 Phone UI, Search, Food/Beverage, Compass

## Added

- Swipe left/right across the app shell to move between tabs. Inputs, buttons, selects, and Leaflet map gestures are ignored so controls still work.
- Hero search bar now filters route cards by name, place, difficulty, route type, and tags, then jumps to Explore.
- Health food catalog expanded with more trail foods.
- Beverage catalog added with water/electrolyte/common drinks. Drink picks add ounces to water and calories when relevant.
- Field Kit now includes a phone live compass beside the future Sense HAT LED compass block.

## Phone compass notes

The phone compass uses browser `deviceorientation` / `deviceorientationabsolute` events. On iPhone, the user may need to tap **Enable phone compass** and approve motion/orientation permission. Magnetic interference, cases, vehicles, and nearby electronics can skew readings. The Sense HAT compass remains a later hardware integration.
