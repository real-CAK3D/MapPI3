# MapPi3 V1.2.19 Launch Field Plan

## Added

- Launch water, food/calorie, phone battery, and power-bank quantity fields.
- Route/weather/hiker based estimates for suggested water and calories.
- Editable expected return time.
- Local emergency contact and check-in note.
- Share/export hike plan as a text file.
- Readiness log records return time, water, calories, and emergency contact.
- `mappi3.launchPlan` persists the field plan locally.

## Safety

Water/calorie estimates are rough field-planning aids only. Adjust for terrain, heat, humidity, medical needs, local rules, and actual trail conditions.
